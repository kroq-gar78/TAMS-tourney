Frequently Asked Questions

Registration

How many tests am I allowed to take?

Each student is allowed to sign up for as many tests as he/she would like as long as the time periods for these tests do not overlap. Please refer to the schedule for event timings.

How much is registration?

Fees are $10 for individuals. When registering as a school, the fee is $40 for every 5 students. Lunch is included in the fees.

When do I pay the registration fee?

Payment for TAMS Tournament will be due on November 7th, 2015. Please pay the fee at the door.

When is the last day to register online?

Online registration is due on November 6, which is the day before the competition. Walk-ins, however, are allowed.

Are walk-in competitors allowed?

Yes, walk-ins are allowed. Fees are due at the door.

Testing

Where can I find information about parking, the schedule, and room numbers?

This information will be posted at a later date.

What happens if I miss my test/arrive late?

First off, please please please try to on time to your test. Proctors will not wait around for students to show up and will administer tests promptly. If you do happen to arrive late, you may take the test but will NOT receive any extra time or other compensation for being late. Students who miss the test will not be permitted to take the test at a later time.

Do you require answers to be written in ink or pencil?

All tests permit the use of pencil or any other writing utensil EXCEPT the Number Sense test which must be written in ink (preferably not red).

I’ve written the correct answer but I didn’t receive any credit… why?

All answers should be recorded legibly in the space provided. An illegible answer is the same as a wrong answer and receives 0 points. Answers not written in the appropriate format is also considered wrong and will receive 0 points. Remember to rationalize all radicals and to express fractional answers as improper fractions (not mixed numbers or decimals) in simplest form unless specified otherwise.

How many points do I receive for each correct answer?

Point values will be assigned for each question. If no point values are assigned, assume all questions are weighted equally. More detailed information about individual test scoring will be provided before the test.

Is there a penalty for guessing?

Nope! So if you’re lucky, guess away!

Am I allowed to use any aids during the test?

No books or study aids are allowed. All cell phones, computers, and other electronics must be put away.

What kind and when can I use a calculator?

Calculators are not allowed for math tests (individual or team); however, a four-function, scientific, or graphing calculators will be allowed on science tests. Cell phones are NOT to be used as calculators

What is your policy on Calculator Watches?

Calculator watches, though stylish, function as a calculator and are not permitted on math tests. You may use a calculator watch on the science test; however, it is not recommended.
